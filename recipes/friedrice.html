Fried Rice

Description of a burger

Fried rice is a dish found all over different parts of Asia, and consists primarily of rice, and any assortment of meat, egg and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white long-grain rice
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 bacon rashers, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 2 shallots, trimmed, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a large saucepan of boiling water for 12 minutes or until tender. Drain and leave to cool.
  2. Heat oil in non-stick wok or large frying pan over medium heat. Add eggs. Swirl over base to form an omelette. Cook for 2 minutes or until set. Transfer to a chopping board. Set aside to cool slightly. Cut into short strips.
  3. Add bacon to wok. Cook 4 minutes until light golden. Add carrot. Stir fry 1 minute. Add sdhallots, peas and rice. Cook, stirring, 3-4 minutes. Add eff and soy sauce. Stir until heated through. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and top with extra shallots. Serve immediately.
